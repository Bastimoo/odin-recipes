How to make basic salad.

This Recipe explains how to make a basic salad

It will look like this:



Ingredients

You will need the following ingredients

  • 500g green salad
  • 100g tomatoes
  • Sauce

Steps

You will need to follow the following steps:
  1. Cut the ingredients up
  2. Put the ingredients in a bowl
  3. Mix the ingredients
  4. Drain them in sauce
